About Urban Dance Leipzig

This is a non-profit project aiming to support the growth of the urban dance community in Leipzig, managed by a small team. It's not meant to be a platform for advertising. Rather, this site offers an overview of carefully selected events and classes which contribute to the urban dancing scene. This includes the dance styles Hip Hop, House, Popping, Locking, Breakdane, Voguing, Waacking, Krump, Dancehall and Afro.
